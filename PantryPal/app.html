Egg Fried Rice

Vegetarian ⏱ 20 min Easy 2 servings
Ingredients
Cooked rice2 cups
Eggs2 pcs
Tomato, diced1 pc
Soy sauce, salt, oilto taste
Steps
1

Heat oil in a wok on medium-high heat.

2

Beat and scramble eggs until just cooked, set aside.

3

Stir-fry diced tomato 2 min, add rice and mix well.

4

Add eggs back, season with soy sauce and serve hot.
