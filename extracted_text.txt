Project Overview</strong></h1><p>PantryPal is an AI-powered mobile application that helps users decide what to cook based on ingredients they already have. Users enter available ingredients, select dietary preferences and cuisine type, and the app uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate 3 personalized recipe suggestions with full step-by-step instructions.</p><h2><strong>1.1 Problem Statement</strong></h2><p>Every day, people open their fridge and ask: "What can I cook with this?" Existing apps require searching by recipe name. PantryPal flips this: you tell it what you HAVE, and it tells you what to MAKE.</p><h2><strong>1.2 Solution</strong></h2><p>PantryPal uses real-time AI (Claude API) to analyze available ingredients and generate creative, practical recipes tailored to the user's diet, cuisine preference, and available cook time. No internet cookbook browsing needed.</p><h1><strong>2.
